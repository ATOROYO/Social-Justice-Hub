Transparency in Governance

Governance transparency is crucial for ensuring that citizens, especially the youth, have access to important information and can participate in decision-making. This page offers interactive resources to help young people engage with governance, promote accountability, and take action.

Why Transparency Matters

Transparency ensures that government actions are visible to the public. It reduces corruption, enhances trust, and allows citizens to hold leaders accountable.
